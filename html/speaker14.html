Sio Kee Hong

Sio Kee Hong is Deputy CEO of Tzu Chi Foundation Malaysia. Kee Hong graduated with a Bachelor of Electrical Engineering (Honours) from University Technology Malaysia and obtained his Master of Business Administration from Ohio University, USA. He was attached to two public listed companies and had held roles as Chief Operating Officer and Director of Operations.

As a volunteer, he found a sense of fulfilment in helping others and seeing his spiritual life developed. He began to assume heavier responsibilities in the foundation and 7 years ago, he decided to quit his career and had since serve voluntarily as the deputy CEO for Tzu Chi Foundation KL-Selangor Chapter.

蘇祈逢 - 慈濟基金會雪隆分會副執行長

1992年，蘇祈逢畢業于馬來西亞工藝大學（University Technology Malaysia, UTM）電子工程系，並取得荣誉學士學位。1998年，獲得美国俄亥俄大學（Ohio University）工商管理碩士學位。 他曾任職于兩家上市公司，擔任首席營運員和營運總監。

作為一名志願工作者，在助人的過程中，他感受到內心的富足，找到了人生的意義及方向。因此，七年前决定辭去工作， 開始承擔更重要的責任，義務擔任慈濟基金會雪隆分會副執行長。
