Mr. Muhammad Faisal Abdul Aziz

Mr. Muhammad Faisal (born 1986) is the President of Muslim Youth Movement Malaysia (ABIM). He studied Laws (LLB) and Syariah at the International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM) from 2006-2011. Later he pursued his master degree in Laws (LLM) at Mara Technology University.

Among his passions as the President of ABIM is to constantly engage with interfaith organizations, Advocating Reform Agenda and Sustainable Development Goals.

He is a coordinator for Project Bangsa Malaysia and Malaysian Interfaith Climate Change Network (MICCN). He is also the author of the book "Islam and the Future of Reformist Thought" and "Cosmopolitan Islam & The Forging of Bangsa Malaysia".
